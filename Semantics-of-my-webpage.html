Our school

Alordhara School is a vibrant and inclusive educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. Nestled in the heart of Uttara,Dhaka, our school offers a diverse curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students from kindergarten through 12th grade. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing a supportive and nurturing environment, encouraging students to reach their full potential. With state-of-the-art facilities, a wide range of extracurricular activities, and a strong emphasis on community engagement, Alordhara prepares students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.
